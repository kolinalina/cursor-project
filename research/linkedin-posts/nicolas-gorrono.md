## Post 4
**Date:** 2026-04-19

Google just finished rolling out the March 2026 Core Update.

People are losing traffic and do not know why, but the pattern is clear: older blog posts are now being scored against five trust signals.

Miss them, and rankings drop.

Five fixes:

1. **Author Bio:** Make E-E-A-T visible with a real person and relevant credentials.
2. **Sources:** Back every claim with studies, data, and original reporting.
3. **Information Gain:** Add something new; repeated summaries are easy to ignore.
4. **Schema Markup:** Make trust signals machine-readable through structured data.
5. **Original Data:** Use screenshots, case studies, survey results, and proof of real work.
