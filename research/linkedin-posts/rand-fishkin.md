## Post 1
**Date:** 2026-04-23

Should you spam AI tools? Use gray-hat SEO techniques? Publish listicles where you rank yourself #1, run dozens of Reddit accounts to push a narrative, or pay for placements on the right domains with the right content chunking to appear on top of AI Overviews?

For 20 years, I was a staunch proponent of white-hat-only SEO.

Now, after Google and the rest of Big Tech have shown themselves to be far more harmful than many black-hat actors ever were, my view is different: as long as it does not harm your business, you have no obligation to be ethical toward giant corporations that would never return the favor.

Clip from my interview with Danny Goodwin for Search Engine Land's 20-year anniversary.
